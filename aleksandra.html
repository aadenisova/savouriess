About

I am a bioinformatician with a passion for solving complex problems at the intersection of technology and biology. My journey began in backend development at Yandex, where I honed my technical skills and learned to build robust systems that can handle real-world challenges.

Today, I focus on innovation and problem-solving in avian research, working on cutting-edge projects that push the boundaries of what's possible in computational biology. I also contribute to commercial research at Genotek, where I apply my expertise to advance genomic technologies.

Beyond my professional work, I am deeply passionate about creative expression through writing poetry and stories about my travels and experiences. I believe that the intersection of science and art creates the most meaningful insights, and I strive to bring this perspective to everything I do.
